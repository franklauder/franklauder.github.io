For my project, I opted to amalgamate domestic flight data spanning from 2009 to 2018. The combined dataset comprises sixty million rows, offering a substantial dataset for distributed computing. To handle this extensive dataset, I chose to utilize Amazon Web Services EMR.
The project is divided into two Jupyter notebooks. The initial section focuses on feature engineering and data exploration, while the second part involves applying various classification models to predict the flight arrival status.
