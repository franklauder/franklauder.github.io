The idea of this project began with how information was provided to the Finance office for budgeting purposes.  What was
provded was projections based on previous years disbursements which was at best a guess.  Direct Loans are the
largest source of revenue for the college thus an accurate picture of funding not based on an educatd gueess was needed.  For a realistic picture of future Direct Loan disbursements I turned to Time Series methods.  With these technighues I forecast Direct Loan disbursments a year into the future provdiding solid information to work with.  The project can be accessed from the link below.  For those who are interested in the code used for this project check out my Direct Loan Forecasting Project repository on Github.
